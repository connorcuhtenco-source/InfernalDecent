The Curse

You are a fallen warrior whose soul has been dragged into Purgatory.

Upon awakening before the rusted gates of the underworld, you discover a terrible truth:

"The only way out is down."

A mysterious curse binds countless souls to Hell. To escape, you must descend through the layers of the underworld, harvest souls from the damned, grow stronger, and destroy the source of the curse itself.

Three seals. Three guardians. One truth buried beneath it all.
