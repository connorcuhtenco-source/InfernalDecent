The Broken Curse

Act I — The Descent

You are a fallen warrior whose soul has been dragged into the depths of Purgatory. Your spirit, however, refuses to break.

Awakening at the rusted iron gates of the underworld, you discover a terrible truth — the only way out is down.

To escape damnation, you must descend through the layers of Hell, harvest the souls of the damned, and destroy the source of the curse itself.

✦ ✦ ✦

Layer I — Gates of Despair

A silent grey labyrinth guarded by wandering, mindless Husks. At its edge stands The Gatekeeper — a towering, armoured brute whose axe has shattered a thousand souls.

✦ ✦ ✦

Layer II — Fiery Catacombs

A scorching landscape of shifting stone and molten rivers. Rabid Hellhounds hunt in packs. Deep within lies Alpha Cerberus — three heads, one purpose: your destruction.

✦ ✦ ✦

Act III — Throne of Damnation

You breach the obsidian gates. The final gauntlet begins. The Overlord of Hell awaits on his throne of ash and bone.

✦ ✦ ✦

The Ending

"I was never your enemy. I was your prison."

The curse shatters. Every trapped soul rises toward light. The warrior ascends — and finally escapes damnation as the underworld crumbles into nothingness.

About Infernal Descent

You are a fallen warrior whose soul has been dragged into the depths of Purgatory. Your spirit refuses to break. The only way out is down — through three layers of Hell, harvesting souls to grow stronger, until you face the Ruler of the Underworld himself.

Act I — The Descent

Awaken at the rusted iron gates. Complete the tutorial, defeat the beginner boss, and claim your tutorial weapon.

Act II — The Trials of Hell

  • Layer 1: The Gates of Despair — Grey labyrinth, Husks, and The Gatekeeper.
  • Layer 2: The Fiery Catacombs — Molten rivers, Hellhounds, and Alpha Cerberus.

Act III — The Throne of Damnation

Breach the obsidian gates. Cyclops, Gargoyles, and the final multi-phase battle against The Devil await.
