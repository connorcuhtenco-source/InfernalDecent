The Broken Curse

You are a fallen warrior whose soul has been dragged into Purgatory. Awakening at the rusted iron gates of the underworld, you discover that the only way out is down.

To escape damnation, you must descend through the layers of Hell, harvest the souls of the damned, grow stronger, and destroy the source of the curse itself.

“I was never your enemy. I was your prison.”
