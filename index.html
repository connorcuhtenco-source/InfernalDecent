STORY

The Only Way Out Is Down

You are a fallen warrior whose soul has been dragged into Purgatory. Your spirit refuses to break.

To escape damnation, you must descend through three layers of Hell, harvest souls, claim cursed weapons, and destroy the source of the curse.

Defeat the Gate Keeper, Alpha Cerberus, and finally the Devil himself. Break the curse and free every trapped soul.

INSTRUCTIONS

How To Survive

Movement

WASD Move around the dungeon

SHIFT Dash / sprint using stamina

Combat

SPACE Attack with your weapon

E Use your special ability

Defense

Q Hold to block with your shield

Blocking reduces damage but drains stamina.

Progression

R Open the skill tree

Every 5 souls gives 1 skill point.
