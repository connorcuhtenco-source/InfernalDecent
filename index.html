The Curse

You are a fallen warrior whose soul has been dragged into the depths of Purgatory. However, your spirit refuses to break.

Awakening at the rusted iron gates of the underworld, you discover that the only way out is down.

To escape damnation, you must descend through the layers of Hell, harvest the souls of the damned to grow stronger, and destroy the source of the curse itself.

Layer I — Gates of Despair. A silent grey labyrinth guarded by mindless Husks. At its edge stands The Gatekeeper, a towering armoured brute.

Layer II — Fiery Catacombs. A scorching landscape of shifting stone and molten rivers, hunted by rabid Hellhounds. The Alpha Cerberus guards the second seal.

Layer III — Throne of Damnation. You breach the obsidian gates. The Ruler of the Underworld awaits.
